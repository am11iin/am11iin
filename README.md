### 👁️ &nbsp;Who am I

I'm a **Master 1 student in Visual Computing** at USTHB, Algiers — where I spend my days training machines to perceive and understand images the way humans do.

I work at the intersection of **mathematics, vision and code** — from the algebra of transformations to the pixels on a screen.

Outside academia, I build web interfaces with React and create computer vision experiments that feel alive.

## 🔬 &nbsp;Academic Focus — Visual Computing

> What I study, research and apply every day at USTHB.

| Domain | Topics |
|--------|--------|

| 👁️ **Computer Vision** | Object detection, feature extraction, geometric transformations, optical flow |
| 🧮 **Mathematical Foundations** | Linear algebra, projective geometry, convolution theory |
